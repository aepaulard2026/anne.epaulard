About

I have been a professor of economics at Paris Dauphine University since 2013 and Director of the Economics and Environment Department at OFCE since 2023. I am interested in economic policies, their design, implementation, and evaluation. In the past, I have worked for the International Monetary Fund (2000-2004), the French Treasury (2005-2013), ENSAE (1997-2000), and France Stratégie (2013-2023). I am currently working on the co-benefits of reducing CO2 emissions, the role of local authorities in the transition to a low-carbon economy, an evaluation of fiscal policies in France since 1974, and the effect of the reform of the judicial map on the efficiency of corporate bankruptcy proceedings
